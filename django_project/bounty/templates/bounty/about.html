About

Are you a solo player tired of endless bridge battles? Maybe you're a lone partisan willing to share some juicy intel gathered behind enemy lines. Or a clan man who needs fresh bodies for the front. Or a builder, exasperated by the holes in your defenses. Whatever the case, Foxhole Bounties is your one-stop for coordination!

Post your Bounty with details, and hopefully get paired with someone willing to lend their time and energy to the cause!

Join the fight today!

How do I get started?

1. Create an account.

2. Click on your username in the top-right. This will take you to your profile.

3. Select your team affiliation and hit "save" (Don't try spying on the enemy team, it wont work)

4. Authenticate with Discord (you can only link one Discord account, this is non-reversible)

5. Make sure you are verified on SIGIL if you are a Colonial (WARDEN support in development)

5. Click the VERIFY button on your profile. If you are verified on SIGIL, this will verify your account.

Your verification status resets at the start of every war, so make sure to re-verify at the start of a new war!

Best Practices

Take lots of screenshots

Have Discord messages enabled! This will allow you to reach out to users who accept your bounties, making it easier to coordinate further.

Post multiple pictures of the objective if you can. The more intel you can provide for the accepting party, the better! Map screenshots and in-game images are great.

Use Grid Coordinates

Posting in-game grid coordinates will add a Foxhole map display to your post with the objective marked. You can use the in-game map to find the grid coordinates. A guide exists here.

Be Specific

The Bounty form requires you to select the Bounty Type and Hex of operation. In addition to this, you can post a description of your bounty. The character limit is 2048, so feel free to be as detailed as you want. Write the fanfic milsim operations manual you've always wanted.

Don't accept multiple jobs at the same time. Accept a job only when you are sure you have the time to do it.

Post completions! When you finish a Bounty, submit a completion! It will give you street cred.
